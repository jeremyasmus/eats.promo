ABOUT HOME MEAL KITS & FOOD DELIVERY

A meal kit is a subscription service provided by a company that sends you fresh ingredients and recipes to prepare homecooked meals. The subscription based plan allows you to personalize the meal choices based on dietary needs and preferences. You may chose serving sizes to accommodate 2 persons to a family of 4, with varied price tiers and an option to skip rotations prior to the next delivery.

Food delivery and take-out services allow you to order from restaurants, retailers and grocery stores, while at home or on the go. Want lunch from your favorite local restaurant or need your refrigerator restocked? No problem. If you and your family aren't comfortable with dining in or lack the time to grocery shop for yourself, you can count on the many contactless delivery and/or curbside pick-up options.
